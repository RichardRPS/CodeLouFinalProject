The Camino de Santiago is one of the most famous treks in the world. Thousands of hikers (called Perigrinos) walk the entire 500 mile trek on the French Way every year. This trip will be one experience to remember the rest of your life. If you cannot do the entire trip this year, then some time in the years to come you will definitely return to do so!

The Louisville Presbyterian Seminary will be hosting a trip for Perigrinos in June of 2018. The limit of registrants will be 20. You should be in resonably good shape to make the 80k successfully. Come join us!

The 12 day trip is just $3,000.00
